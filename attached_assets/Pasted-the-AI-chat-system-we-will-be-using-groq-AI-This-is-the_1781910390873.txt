### Key Project Details

- **Current Status:**
  - The live site exists but was developed by another, now unreachable developer.
  - The project will be redeveloped from scratch based on the existing live site and updated Figma designs.
  - A quote is requested for the redesign and redevelopment.

- **Platform Concept:**
  - Initially conceived as an immigration help platform connecting users to immigration agents.
  - Revised to focus on job seekers providing access to **career advisors**, **job coaches**, or **work specialists**.

- **User Interaction Model:**
  - Users ask questions which first receive an AI-generated response.
  - The AI response is held back and reviewed by a job coach or career specialist.
  - The final answer sent to the user includes **both AI and human responses**.
  - For users to ask a question via chat, they must purchase and spend virtual **coins**.

- **Payment Systems:**
  - Users purchase coins using multiple payment methods:
    - **Card payments**
    - **Cryptocurrency payments** (to accommodate users facing card payment difficulties).
  
- **Communication Modes:**
  - **Chat**: Requires coin purchase; response is a combination of AI and human review.
  - **Call**: Users call a number and are charged via airtime; no coin purchase required.
